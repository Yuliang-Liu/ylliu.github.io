## Basic Information
Professor and Ph.D. Supervisor at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. He has been selected for the National High-Level Talent Special Support Program (Young Top Talent), the CAST Young Talent Promotion Program, and is a recipient of the Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award of the China Society of Image and Graphics (CSIG). He is recognized as an AI 2000 Global Artificial Intelligence Scholar (one of 232 scholars from Mainland China) and is listed among Stanford University’s Top 2% Scientists Worldwide. Recipient of ACL 2024 Best Paper Award.
